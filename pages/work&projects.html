Tuki - Your AI-powered Language Learning App: Tuki is an innovative mobile application that utilizes artificial intelligence to detect highlighted text and perform text recognition. By taking a photo within the app, it automatically identifies words, translates them, and organizes them into a flashcard system for easy language learning. Currently focused on vocabulary learning, Tuki is set to expand its features, allowing users to draw specific images on the photographed sheet, which can be sent to a designated folder within the app or via email for convenient organization.
